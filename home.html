Watering plants can be hard. People get caught up in their busy routine, long days, and difficult lifestyles, often forgetting and procrastinating to water their plants. This ultimately leads to the death of their beloved plant, costing time and money. As plant lovers, we understand the difficulty in remembering to water oneâ€™s plants, so we want to work to propose a solution that can help everyone who loves plants. Our application aims to helps you remember to water your plants on a concurrent basis. Flowers 4 Lyfe will provide you with notifications and detailed information, regarding the amount of water needed on a plant by plant basis, and how often watering is required. We aim to help every user remember to water their plants in their busy lives.
